The only way we could feel
close to the girls was
through these impossible
trips which have scarred
us forever, making us
happier with dreams than wives.

Collecting everything we
could of theirs, the Lisbon
girls wouldn't leave our minds.
But they were slipping away.
The colour of their eyes was

fading, along with the locations
of moles and dimples. From five
they had become four, and they
were all living in the dead,
becoming shadows.

We'd have lost them completely
if they hadn't contacted us.
